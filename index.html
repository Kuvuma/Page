The Product

The KUVUMA Smart Speed Bump

The KUVUMA Smart Speed Bump is an intelligent infrastructure device that captures kinetic energy from passing vehicles and converts it into usable electricity — seamlessly integrated into existing road surfaces.

Converts mechanical force into usable energy

Technology

How the Technology Works

01
🚗

Vehicle Passes

The weight of the vehicle activates the mechanism embedded in the road surface.

02
⚙️

Mechanical Motion

The internal system captures and controls the displacement movement precisely.

03
⚡

Energy Conversion

Integrated generators transform mechanical energy into clean electricity.

04
🏙️

Energy Distribution

Electricity is stored in batteries or delivered to nearby infrastructure.
